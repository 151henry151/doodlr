Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-01-01

Doodlr is a collaborative drawing app. We respect your privacy and are committed to being transparent about the data we collect and how it is used.

Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to provide the service:

  • Usage data such as actions performed in the app (e.g., painting pixels)
  • Basic device information required for app functionality and diagnostics

We do not collect sensitive personal information. We do not sell your data.

How We Use Information

  • To operate, maintain, and improve the app experience
  • To prevent abuse and ensure platform security and integrity
  • To diagnose issues and monitor performance

Third-Party Services

Doodlr may use third-party services for analytics, error tracking, or hosting. These providers process data on our behalf and are bound by their own privacy policies.

Data Retention

We retain data only for as long as necessary to provide the service and to comply with legal obligations. You may request deletion of your data where applicable by law.

Children’s Privacy

Doodlr is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
