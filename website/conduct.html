Code of Conduct

We want Doodlr to be a friendly place to create and share pixel art. Please follow these guidelines:

  • Be respectful. Do not post hateful, harassing, or threatening content.
  • No sexually explicit or violent content.
  • No illegal content or content that infringes on others’ rights.
  • Report content that violates this Code of Conduct.

Violations may result in removal of content or other action at our discretion.

Reporting

To report content, use the “Report” action within the app, or email: hello@hromp.com
